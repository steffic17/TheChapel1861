Accommodation at Wensum 1861

Stunning four bedroom converted chapel, spacious, full of character, luxury family home, with parking and EV charging point, in quiet, hidden away town centre location of Fakenham.

4 bedrooms • 3 en-suite • Open plan living area • Garden to the front • Parking space • EV charging.

Sleeps 2 – 8 people • No smoking • One well-behaved dog (downstairs only please)

More about Wensum 1861

Wensum 1861 is a wonderful conversion of what was once the Old Methodist Chapel and later Methodist Hall, in Fakenham. Converted to a high standard in 2018, The Chapel has an impressive open plan living area, with wood burner, well-equipped kitchen, generous dining space and chapel windows. A staircase leads to a vaulted galleried landing and four large bedrooms, all with exposed beams.

Entrance: With cloakroom and coat cupboard.

Living area: The living area is open plan with plenty of natural light from the feature chapel window. With lots of comfy seating, wood burner and FreeView TV. And the dining area can comftably accommodate eight people.

Kitchen: The modern well-equipped kitchen, has a double electric oven and 6-ring gas hob, fridge/freezer, dishwasher, plenty of storgae space and another feature chapel window.

Utility area: Perfect for a quiet space for you canine companion, with a washing machine, laundry and cleaning supplies. Also home to the boiler (off limits to guests please).

Master suite: The master suite, has a super kingsize bed, large dressing room and en-suite with double shower and WC.

Bedroom 2: With kingsize bed and en-suite with shower and toilet.

Bedroom 3: With zip & link beds (twin or kingsize on request) and en-suite with shower and WC.

Bedroom 4: With twin beds.

Family bathroom: With bath and WC.

Outside: The enclosed outside space provides a lovely quiet, sunny space with ample seating and BBQ. There is also parking for a car and EV charging point.

No smoking. We can welcome one small-medium sized well-behaved dog downstairs only.

WiFi • Gas fired central heating • Underfloor heating (ground floor) • Bed linen • Towels • Travel cots (x2) • High chairs (x2) • Wood burner (logs available locally) • EV charging

Local Area

The Location

Wensum 1861 is situated in a hidden town centre location of the sleepy market town of Fakenham. It is within easy walking distance of restaurants, local speciality shops, traditional market, cinema, and bowling alley.

A perfect base to visit all North Norfolk has to offer, including beautiful sandy beaches, bustling seaside villages, grand stately homes, golf courses, sailing facilities, historic King’s Lynn, and the cathedral city of Norwich.
